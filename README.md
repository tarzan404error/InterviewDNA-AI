## 📌 Overview

InterviewDNA AI is an intelligent mock interview platform designed to simulate real-world hiring processes through adaptive questioning, behavioral analysis, performance evaluation, and explainable hiring intelligence.

Unlike traditional interview systems that only score answers, InterviewDNA AI continuously evaluates how a candidate thinks, adapts, communicates, performs under pressure, and responds to changing interview conditions.

The platform generates a unique **Interview DNA Profile**, providing recruiters and candidates with a deeper understanding of interview readiness.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Modern interview preparation platforms often suffer from:

* Generic interview questions
* Static difficulty levels
* Subjective feedback
* Lack of behavioral insights
* No pressure simulation
* Poor interview readiness measurement

As a result, candidates receive scores but rarely understand:

> Why they performed the way they did.

InterviewDNA AI addresses this challenge through adaptive evaluation, explainable scoring, integrity validation, and behavioral analytics.

# 🚀 Key Innovations

## 🧠 Interview DNA Engine

Generates a behavioral intelligence profile instead of a simple interview score.

Tracks:

* Knowledge Index
* Communication Index
* Adaptability Index
* Confidence Index
* Consistency Index
* Pressure Handling Index

---

## ⚡ Adaptive Interview Engine

Interview difficulty dynamically evolves:

```text
Easy
 ↓
Medium
 ↓
Hard
```

Strong candidates face increasingly challenging questions while weaker candidates receive supportive progression.

---

## 🔥 Pressure Mode

When a candidate consistently performs well:

* Difficulty increases
* Response time decreases
* Scenario-based questions activate

This simulates realistic interview pressure.

---

## 🛡️ Integrity Detection Layer

Detects:

* Fake resumes
* Fake job descriptions
* Non-resume document uploads
* Plagiarism
* Suspicious response timing
* AI-generated inconsistencies

---

## 📊 Explainable AI Evaluation

Every score includes transparent reasoning.

Evaluation dimensions:

* Accuracy
* Clarity
* Depth
* Relevance
* Time Efficiency

No black-box scoring.
